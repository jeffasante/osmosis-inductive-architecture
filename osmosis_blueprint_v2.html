THREE COUPLED CRUXES

  

    

      
CRUX 1

      
DSL bias

      
Wrong grammar makes everything downstream look worse than it is. Language design is as important as the inference algorithm.

    

    

      
CRUX 2

      
Proposal calibration

      
Continuous geometry must align with discrete behavioral equivalence classes — smooth embeddings that don't respect program semantics are useless for search.

    

    

      
CRUX 3

      
Bridge stability

      
Gradients must flow from verified programs back to the encoder. Risk: model collapses into a fancy heuristic ranker without learning causal abstractions.
